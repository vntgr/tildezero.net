Free yourself [2023-01-26]

If you free your thinking mind,
your thoughts eventually will go away;

If you practice patience,
you eventually will see time,
as a great teacher in your life;

If you practice patience,
you eventually will see time,
as a great teacher in your life;

If you free yourself, my friend;
you see life as life itself.
